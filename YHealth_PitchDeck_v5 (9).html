Scientific Foundation

Peer-Reviewed Science Underpins Every Assessment

Every measurement YHealth delivers is grounded in published, validated research — not wellness marketing.

❤️ PPG Physiological Age (Patented)

  • Photoplethysmography captures pulse waveform via smartphone camera — validated across 600K+ users on Lifenome platform
  • Correlates with cardiovascular fitness age (VO₂max proxy), vascular stiffness, and autonomic recovery capacity
  • Peer-reviewed: Nitzan et al., Charlton et al., IEEE Signal Processing Letters (2020–2023)
  • FDA-cleared wearable-grade accuracy; Lifenome holds patent on smartphone delivery method

📷 Body Composition Imaging (Patented)

  • AI-powered 3D body composition from a single smartphone photo — visceral fat, muscle mass, BMI, posture index
  • Validated against DEXA scan gold standard (r² > 0.91) across multiple independent clinical trials
  • Published: npj Digital Medicine, JMIR mHealth, Obesity Research & Clinical Practice (2021–2024)
  • Lifenome patent covers multi-angle AI estimation pipeline; deployed across 14 countries

🧬 Polygenic Risk Scores (PRS)

  • 213 disease PRS derived from genome-wide association studies (GWAS) with 1M+ participants each
  • Validated in UK Biobank (500K), All of Us (650K), FinnGen (500K) population cohorts
  • Risk prediction AUC 0.70–0.88 across cardiovascular, metabolic, oncology, and neurological domains
  • Published standard: Lambert et al., Nature Reviews Genetics (2021); Wand et al., Nature Medicine (2021)

⏱️ Epigenetic Biological Age Clocks

  • 4 validated methylation-based clocks: Horvath (2013), Hannum (2013), PhenoAge, and GrimAge
  • GrimAge: strongest predictor of all-cause mortality and morbidity (HR 1.16 per year of acceleration)
  • Analysis of 850,000 CpG sites from standard blood draw — gold-standard longitudinal aging biomarker
  • Lu et al., Nature Aging (2019); Levine et al., Aging (2018) — most-cited epigenetic aging papers
