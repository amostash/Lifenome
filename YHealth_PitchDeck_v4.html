YESS Health (Non-Profit)

Owns member data rights. Governs consent. Distributes 40% of net revenue back to local Y associations. Represents mission alignment to regulators and grant bodies.

YHealth Commercial (LLC)

Holds investor equity. Operates subscriptions, marketplace, SaaS licensing, and B2B commercial contracts. White-label OpCo for health systems and employers.

Lifenome Technology

Provides AI platform, multi-omics engine, app development, kiosk software, and data infrastructure. Holds 20% equity in YHealth LLC (YMCA USA 80%). Receives platform fee ($50K/mo → $100K/mo) credited against quarterly rev share; permanent 20% of subscription/marketplace net + 30% of data licensing — rights survive any termination.
